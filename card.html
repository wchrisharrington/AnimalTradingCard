Peregrine Falcon

The peregrine is the fastest member of the animal kingdom, reaching speeds of up to 200 miles per hour!

  • Scientific Name: Falco peregrinus
  • Average Wingspan: 38 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 15.5 years
  • Top Speed: 242 miles per hour

Peregrine falcons can be found world wide, with a breeding range that includes land regions from the Arctic tundra to the tropics. Because of their fantastic hunting skills, they are a very popular bird used in falconry. Peregrines hunt primarily other birds, which they catch by diving at speeds over 200 miles per hour!
